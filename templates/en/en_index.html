Electoral Programme

2023-2027

SOLUCIONS IN SERVICES AND SECURITY


Clean Sitges: The budget for gardening, street cleaning, and beach maintenance will be doubled.

Safe Sitges: 90 new service agents will be deployed in all neighborhoods 24/7 to protect all groups. A monitored parking area for motorcycles, bicycles, and scooters will be established in each neighborhood.

Sitges without squatters: Zero tolerance. A new office will be created to assist property owners, providing free rapid intervention 24/7 to evict illegal occupants, along with social assistance if needed.

Sitges with better services: A mobile complaint vehicle and a citizen service office (OAC) in every neighborhood. The historically slow process of issuing business and urban planning permits will be resolved, and a new police office in Sitges will handle the issuance and renewal of ID cards, residence permits, and passports.

SOLUCIONS IN SUSTAINABILTY

Eco Sitges: Charging stations for electric vehicles will be installed in all neighborhoods. Electric vehicle owners will be exempt from taxes. Property tax (IBI) will be waived for homes using renewable energy. Public transportation will be free.

More accessible and connected Sitges: 100% free tolls on the Garraf C-32 tunnels and connections to Cubelles and Baix Penedès.

Sitges with public restrooms and drinking fountains: Installed in every neighborhood.

Sitges committed to biodiversity and pets: Increased funding for animal shelters and pro-nature NGOs. Three beaches will be designated year-round for pet-friendly enjoyment, and new fenced dog parks with play areas will be built in every neighborhood.

SOLUCIONS IN TOURISM AND COMMERCE

Revitalizing quality tourism and commerce: Municipal technical resources will be tripled to attract quality events and new tourists throughout the year, strengthening the local economy. A new Business Centre HUB will be created as a large startup incubator.

Agreement with street vendors: They will be relocated away from the seafront, prioritizing local commerce.

Revitalizing nightlife: Ten new music venues will be opened in the “Street of Sin,” relocating current restaurants. Two new large nightclubs will be built, one exclusively for minors.

Restoring Sitges' beaches: Submerged breakwaters will be built, and beaches will be replenished with the necessary sand. Hours for beach bars and water sports activities will be extended.

SOLUCIONS IN EMPLOYMENT AND SOCIAL ASSISTANCE

More jobs and social aid in Sitges: The local job market will triple, along with employment programs and social assistance for registered residents.

24-hour pharmacy and medical center (CAP) services: Facilities will be expanded and improved.

Support for senior citizens: Two senior centers will be renovated, and two new centers will be built in different neighborhoods, including adapted sports parks for older adults.

Inclusive Sitges: The entire city will be adapted for accessible and inclusive mobility, and accessible sports and cultural events will be promoted.

SOLUCIONS IN INFRASTRUCTURES, SPORT, DWELLING AND CULTURE

Free parking in Sitges: A large parking facility with 3,000 free spaces will be built. The free blue zone in the Oasis neighborhood will be maintained.

More sports facilities: Six new artificial turf soccer fields, four basketball courts, four paddle courts, and four tennis courts, all with nighttime lighting. A new municipal sports center (also usable as a concert venue), an indoor track and field facility, and multipurpose courts for hockey, futsal, artistic skating, and rhythmic gymnastics. Four beach volleyball courts and two outdoor workout parks on the beaches. A high-quality children’s park in every neighborhood and a large indoor children’s park for winter. A new municipal swimming pool and gym with affordable prices, promoting competitive Olympic sports and local teams.

More affordable housing: 1,000 new social housing units will be built, with rent capped at €450 per month, including 150 units for senior citizens.

Cultural and educational initiatives: More exhibitions and concerts featuring local artists, with performances in public spaces. Former major festivals and events will be revived. Municipalization of early childhood centers and the creation of a new Interpretation Center for Sitges’ Popular Festivals.

ABOUT OUR CANDIDATE

Fernando Llombart, candidate for mayor of Sitges, is a leader committed to the future of our town. With extensive experience and a deep understanding of local challenges, he envisions a more sustainable, inclusive, and prosperous Sitges. His vision and approachability make him the best choice to lead our community towards a better future.
